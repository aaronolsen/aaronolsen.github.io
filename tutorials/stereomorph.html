StereoMorph Tutorial

This tutorial will show you how to use the StereoMorph R package to collect 3D shape data from a stereo camera or video setup. This includes instructions for installation of StereoMorph, making a checkerboard, measuring a checkerboard's square size, camera calibration, digitizing photographs and video frames, 3D reconstruction, shape reflection and alignment, and video frame extraction.
